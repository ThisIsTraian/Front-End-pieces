Cherry Pie Recipe

A delightful cherry pie with a flaky crust and juicy cherry filling, perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 500g pie crusts (homemade or store-bought)
  • 600g fresh or frozen cherries, pitted
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 60g cornstarch
  • 15ml lemon juice
  • 2.5ml almond extract
  • 1.25ml salt
  • 15g unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • Egg wash: 1 large egg beaten with 15ml milk
  • Sugar, for sprinkling (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 218°C (425°F). If using homemade pie crusts, prepare them now.
  2. In a large bowl, gently toss the cherries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, almond extract, and salt together until thoroughly combined.
  3. Place one pie crust into a 23cm (9-inch) pie dish. Spoon the cherry mixture into the crust. Dot with butter. Place the second pie crust over the filling. Trim and crimp edges as desired.
  4. Brush the top crust with egg wash and sprinkle with sugar if desired. Cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake the pie for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  6. Cool the pie for at least 4 hours before slicing and serving.
